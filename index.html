    How it works
    Dijkstra's algorithm (Rust/WASM) on the BNA street graph.
    Edge cost = length × LTS penalty × hill penalty
    (LTS1=1×, LTS2=2×, LTS3=10×)
    Hill: >4%=1.5×, >8%=4×, >12%=50×

    Grade data: SRTM30m via OpenTopoData (baked in).
